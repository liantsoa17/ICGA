About ICGA

The India-Connect Global Alumni Network (ICGA) is a pioneering platform dedicated to strengthening ties between Indian and African professionals, students, and entrepreneurs. We believe in the power of shared knowledge, mutual growth, and collaborative success. Through our comprehensive services, we're building a bridge that connects opportunities, fosters innovation, and creates lasting impact across two dynamic continents.
